..and Dim Sum

Hello World! I love to eat, exercise, learn, play, and enjoy life! My favorite food(s) is dim sum. Dim sum is an assortment of Cantonese-style refreshments including dumplings and other tasty options.

This is an image of Guangdong, where dim sum originates from. China is a geographically large country, so diversity in food is common to see when you compare cuisines from the south (Guangdong) and the north (Beijing).

Beijing is famous for their Peking Duck!

I love Chinese food! There is so much variety and flavor, and it's also something that reminds me of home and other warm, great feelings.
